Simple Pizza Recipe This homemade pizza is easy to make and full of flavor. The dough is soft and chewy, topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings like olives, mushrooms, and pepperoni. Bake it in the oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal!


    Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

    Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, then mix until a dough forms.
