Salad



Description

A light and refreshing salad made with fresh vegetables, olive oil, and lemon juice. Perfect as a side dish or a healthy meal.


    Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 onion (sliced)
  • 1 lettuce (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

    Steps

  1. Put all chopped vegetables in a bowl.
  2. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
